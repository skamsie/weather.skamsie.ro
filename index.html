INFO

SEARCH INPUT
For big cities or locations with a unique name, providing only the name usually suffices. For less known places, or places with the same name as a county, it is better to provide also the county, a zip code, region and / or country.

GEOLOCATION
Click to show weather information for your current location. The button is hidden on browsers that do not support geolocation.

UNITS
Toggling the units button (°C / °F) will also switch between the international system and the imperial system for the temperature and wind speed information. The units are changed only after submitting.

AIR QUALITY
Air quality index is always displayed. The data is taken from the closest station to the searched location, which can be at a considerable distance. You can check the 'Air quality location' field and decide on its relevance.

COMPARE
Using the button labeled + a second weather search area can be added to compare weather from two locations simultaneously. This button is hidden on small mobile devices.



About

This website displays weather and air quality information for up to two locations at once.
